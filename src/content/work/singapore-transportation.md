## Role
**DevOps TechLead** at FPT Software

## Duration
2023 - 2024

## Overview
Led a team of 10 engineers in the Cloud Center of Excellence (CoE) for a major Singapore transportation client. Designed and implemented AWS Kubernetes landing zone, modernized CI/CD pipelines, and built comprehensive observability platform.

## Key Achievements

- **Team Leadership**: Led team of 10 in Cloud Center of Excellence
- **Cost Optimization**: Designed AWS EKS landing zone and migrated all workloads from ECS to EKS, **cutting infrastructure costs by 60%**
- **CI/CD Modernization**: Built CI/CD pipelines with Tekton, **reducing AWS CodePipeline costs by 40%**
- **Observability Platform**: Implemented full-stack observability with Grafana Stack and OpenTelemetry

## Technologies
- **Cloud**: AWS (EKS, VPC, IAM, CloudWatch)
- **IaC**: Terraform
- **Container Orchestration**: Kubernetes (EKS)
- **CI/CD**: Tekton, ArgoCD
- **Observability**: OpenTelemetry, Jaeger, Grafana, Prometheus, Loki
- **GitOps**: ArgoCD

## Impact
- **60% cost reduction** through ECS to EKS migration
- **40% CI/CD cost savings** by replacing CodePipeline with Tekton
- Improved deployment frequency from weekly to multiple times per day
- Reduced mean time to recovery (MTTR) with comprehensive observability
- Established cloud-native best practices for the organization
